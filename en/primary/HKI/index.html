Non-binding The least supported lists will concede before the actual election

8 lists

The pro-democracy camp is aiming for 4 seats in the official election and the primaries is non-binding, meaning that given whatever results for the primaries, lists can still continue to campaign for the actual 2020 LegCo election. However, before the actual election in September, a polling will be conducted by the HK PORI (Hong Kong Public Opinion Research Institute) and the least supported list(s) will concede from the actual election, meaning that on the election day, there will only be 4 lists targeting 4 seats.
